## What I Build

AI Engineer focused on building systems where:

- outputs are **grounded in real data**
- behavior is **observable and testable**
- failures are **explainable, not hidden**

I focus on **applied AI systems** where language models are one component within a larger, controlled system.

The goal is not just generation, but **reliable system behavior under real-world conditions**.

## Design Philosophy

- **Structured reasoning** — prefer explicit representations such as graphs, schemas, constraints, and tests over opaque stacks.
- **Language as interface** — use NLP as a control and specification surface for complex systems, not only as open-ended chat.
- **Determinism where it matters** — bias toward pipelines, evaluations, and failure modes you can inspect before production.
- **Deployment-first thinking** — optimize for behavior under load, drift, and partial observability, especially in real-world coordination-heavy systems.

## System Characteristics

- Deterministic pipelines over generative guessing
- Explicit representations over hidden heuristics
- Failure-aware system design with observable behavior
- Built across analytics, execution tracing, and policy reasoning

## System Depth

- Designed and built multiple end-to-end AI systems across API, UI, and execution pipelines
- Systems include APIs, SDKs, execution pipelines, and user interfaces
- Focus on correctness, traceability, and failure handling
- Experience across analytics, execution tracing, and structured retrieval systems

## Collaboration

Interested in real-world problems where constraints are clear, iterations are measurable, and the system has to hold up outside the lab.

I'm interested in working with people building systems where correctness, reasoning, and real-world constraints matter.
